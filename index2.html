The goal of Great Science For All® is to provide science educational opportunities for children in grades Pre-K through 5th grade that would otherwise be unable to afford such programs.

The Mission of this Non-Profit Corporation is to provide enhanced science education for low socio-economic and disadvantaged students. The goal is to eliminate the achievement gap and provide more career opportunities for these children in the STEAM fields.
